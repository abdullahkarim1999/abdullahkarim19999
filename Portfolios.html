The Goal of a Portfolio

The goal of a portfolio is to help you make money. It shows you what you're invested in, how much everything is worth, and all the current market data that you want it to show. It is a way in which the flow of your money is shown, portraying strengths and weaknesses within itself for improvement and efficiency.
